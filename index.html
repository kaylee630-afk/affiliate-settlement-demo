FAQ
Questions you might have.

💡 Do I need to know crypto?

No. If you can use PayPal, you can use PayFlash. We handle the blockchain part — you just enter an amount and click send.

🔐 Is my money safe?

PayFlash never holds your money. Your wallet, your keys. Every transfer requires your signature. The smart contract is open-source and auditable.

💱 How do I convert USDC to my currency?

Use any exchange (Coinbase, Binance, OKX) to swap USDC to your local currency. Or keep it as USDC — it's 1:1 with the US dollar.

💰 What does it cost?

Base L2 gas is ~$0.003 per transaction. That's it. No monthly fees, no percentage cuts, no hidden FX markups.
